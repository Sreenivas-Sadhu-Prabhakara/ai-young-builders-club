## Why this club exists

Kids are already surrounded by AI — in their games, their feeds, their homework help.
Most of them treat it as magic. This club replaces the magic with **understanding and
confidence**: they learn what AI really is, use it deliberately and safely, build
something real with it, and learn to spot when it's wrong.

It's not a lecture series. **Every session ends with the kid's own project further
along.** By Week 4 they stand up and demo their own AI assistant.

## The capstone: build your own AI assistant

The whole club is one project, built in layers:

```
Week 1  →  Pick a PURPOSE      (a homework buddy? a joke bot? a pet-care helper?)
Week 2  →  Design a PERSONALITY (how it talks + its first "system prompt")
Week 3  →  Bring it to CODE     (running in a real Python notebook)
Week 4  →  Make it SAFE & SHOW  (add a guardrail, then demo it)
```
