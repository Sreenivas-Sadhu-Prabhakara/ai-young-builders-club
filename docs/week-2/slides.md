### Slide 1 — Title

### Slide 2 — The whole secret of a chatbot

> ## A chatbot just **predicts the next word**.
> "Twinkle twinkle little ____" → ⭐
> It guesses the next word, then the next, then the next — super fast.

**Say:** "That's it. That's the magic trick. No little person inside — it's a giant **next-word
predictor**. You just played one in the hook."

---

### Slide 3 — The words we'll use

> - **Chatbot** = an AI you talk to in words
> - **LLM** = the giant 'next-word predictor' behind it
> - **Prompt** = what *you* type to steer it
> - **System prompt** = its hidden 'character sheet'

**Say:** "Four words. The chatbot is the thing you talk to, the LLM is the engine, the prompt
is your steering wheel, and the system prompt is the secret personality you'll write today."

---

### Slide 4 — Your prompt is the steering wheel

> ## Better prompt → better answer 🚀
> 😴 "Tell me about dogs."
> 🚀 "You are a fun tutor. List 5 dog facts for an 11-year-old, one sentence each."
> **R-T-F-C: Role · Task · Format · Check**

**Say:** "The AI can't read your mind — it only has your words. Add a **Role**, a **Task**, a
**Format**, and a **Check**, and the answer gets way better. You'll prove it in a minute."

---

### Slide 5 — It can be confidently WRONG

> ## Hallucination 😵
> Ask it about "the Great Sock War of 1850"…
> and it will **invent the whole thing — confidently**.

**Say:** "It predicts words that *sound* right — even when there's no real answer. It's not
lying, it's guessing. So: **always check important stuff.**"

---

### Slide 6 — It can be UNFAIR

> ## Bias 🪞
> It learned from **human-made** examples.
> Lopsided examples → lopsided AI. *(Garbage in, garbage out — sound familiar?)*

**Say:** "Remember last week — good examples, good AI; bad examples, bad AI? Same rule. If the
humans it learned from were lopsided, the chatbot is too."

---

### Slide 7 — Your mission

> ## Today you'll:
> 1. Turn a weak prompt into a strong one (R-T-F-C)
> 2. Catch the bot **making something up**
> 3. Make images — spot the wonky hands & text 🖐️
> **Rule #1: no secrets in prompts!**

**Say:** "Three missions. Steer it, catch it, draw with it. And remember — **never type
secrets into an AI.** Ready? Let's talk to some machines."
