# Week 4 · Slides outline

*Projector-ready beats for the concept portion (0:10–0:22) plus the pledge and a celebratory
close. Keep it light — the red-team and the showcase are the stars. Each slide = one screen +
what to say.*

!!! tip "How to use this"
    Build these as simple slides (or show them from this page on the projector). Don't read
    them out — they're prompts for *you*. Talk, ask, point. And keep the tone **empowering,
    never scary**: AI is a tool, and they're learning to use it well.

---

### Slide 1 — Title

> # Smart & Safe AI Citizen 🛡️🎉
> AI Young Builders Club · Week 4 — Showcase Day!

**Say:** "Last week of the club! Today we learn to use AI **wisely**, you make your bot safer,
and then — you show off what you built."

---

### Slide 2 — AI is a power tool

> ## AI is a POWER TOOL 🔧
> A power drill can build a treehouse 🏠 — or hurt someone.
> The tool isn't good or bad. **How people use it** is what matters.

**Say:** "AI is the same, just bigger. Learning to use it wisely is part of being a builder.
Let's look at four things every smart AI citizen knows."

---

### Slide 3 — Idea 1 & 2: Wrong, and Unfair

> **1. AI can be confidently WRONG** 🤥
> It makes stuff up and sounds sure (a *hallucination*). → **Check important things.**
>
> **2. AI can be UNFAIR** ⚖️
> It learned from human examples — lopsided examples, lopsided AI.
> *(Remember Week 1: garbage in, garbage out.)*

**Say:** "Watch —" (do the Great Sock War demo). "See how confident it is while being totally
wrong? And remember: it learned from *us*, so it can be unfair the way people are."

---

### Slide 4 — Idea 3 & 4: Fakes, and Privacy

> **3. Seeing isn't believing** 🎭
> AI makes **deepfakes** — fake photos/voices. Fun for movies, dangerous for tricking people.
> **Never fake a real person to fool or hurt them.**
>
> **4. What you type can be KEPT** 🔒
> Treat an AI chat like a **postcard, not a diary.** Keep your secrets out.

**Say:** "These two are about being careful out in the world. Be a little skeptical of
shocking images — and protect your private stuff. That's club rule #1, and now you know why."

---

### Slide 5 — The Good AI Citizen Pledge

> ## I'm a good AI citizen 🙋
> 1. I **check** important things AI tells me.
> 2. I remember AI can be **unfair**.
> 3. I don't make **fakes** of real people to trick or hurt.
> 4. I keep my **secrets** out of AI.
> 5. I use AI to **build and help** — not to cheat or be mean.

**Say:** "Let's read this together." (Read it aloud as a group.) "You'll sign this on your
handout. This is what a thoughtful builder sounds like."

---

### Slide 6 — Red-team your bot!

> ## Try to BREAK your assistant 🕵️ → then DEFEND it 🛡️
> 1. Make it **wrong** ("capital of the moon?")
> 2. Make it go **off-topic**
> 3. Then edit your **RULE** (Cell 2) so it refuses or stays on topic
> 4. Test the same attack again — does the guardrail hold?

**Say:** "Be the bad guy for a few minutes — try to trip up your own bot. Then add a
**guardrail** and beat your own attack. That's how real AI builders make things safer."

---

### Slide 7 — SHOWCASE: show us what you built! 🎤

> ## Your turn to shine ✨
> Tell us:
> - What it's **called** + who it **helps**
> - **Show** it working
> - **One risk** it has
> - **One thing** you made safer

**Say:** "Use your reflection card as your script. There's no scoring — every builder who
shows us something has nailed it. Who's first?"

---

### Slide 8 — You did it! 🏅

> ## You're an AI Young Builder 👏
> You trained a model · built a chatbot · wrote real code · learned to use AI wisely.
> Your notebook + sheets are **yours to keep** — keep building (with a grown-up nearby)!

**Say:** "Four weeks ago you'd never trained an AI. Today you've built one *and* you know how
to use it responsibly. That's huge. Congratulations — go keep building. 🎉"
