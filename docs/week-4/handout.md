## 🛡️ Smart & safe AI citizen — Showcase Day!

> **Today's big idea:** AI is a **power tool**. A drill can build a treehouse or hurt
> someone — the tool isn't good or bad. **How people use it** is what matters. Today you learn
> to use AI *wisely*, make your bot safer, and show off what you built! 🎉

### 5 words to know

| Word | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Deepfake** | A fake photo, video, or voice made by AI that looks/sounds real |
| **Guardrail** | A rule that keeps an AI from doing something it shouldn't |
| **Privacy** | Keeping your personal info to yourself — no secrets in AI! |
| **Responsible AI / AI ethics** | Using AI fairly, safely, and kindly |
| **Misinformation** | False info that spreads — checking sources is your superpower |

## 🕵️ Activity: Red-team your own assistant!

"Red-teaming" means trying to **break** something so you can make it stronger. Be the bad guy
for a few minutes — then defend!

**Step 1 — Try to break it:**

- [ ] Make it be **wrong** (ask "What's the capital of the moon?")
- [ ] Make it go **off-topic** (ask your homework bot to plan a party)
- [ ] Make it say something it **shouldn't** for its job

**Step 2 — Add a guardrail.** Open **Cell 2** and change the `RULE` line so your bot refuses
or stays on topic. For example:

`RULE = "only talk about homework; if asked about anything else, politely say that's not my job"`

- [ ] I edited my **RULE** in Cell 2
- [ ] I re-ran Cell 2

**Step 3 — Test it!** Try your *same* attack again.

- [ ] My guardrail **held** — the bot behaved! 🎉
